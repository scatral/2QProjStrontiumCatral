Pasta
Baked macaroni
Jump to recipe!
TIME
2 1/2 hours
YIELD
11"x 8" dish
Baked macaroni is a classic pasta dish that uses red and white pasta sauce. It's usually made during the holiday season, and is a definite crowd pleaser. Though making it is slightly time-consuming, having a plate of some good baked mac is 100% worth it, no matter the occassion:)
What you need
You'll need a decent amount of equipment to make some baked mac, but they're all very simple kitchen tools. You'll need a couple of pots, a baking dish tp add your assembled baked mac in, and some measuring cups and spoons, and a knife.
Some extra tips for making baked macaroni!
When cooking the pasta, make sure to add salt and some oil to the water that you'll use to boil the pasta. This seasons the pasta and stops them from sticking together while cooking. Moreover, since the pasta is still going to be cooked in the oven later on, you may want to just cook it halfway while it's on the stove.
Truffle Pasta Recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 500g macaroni pasta

  • 1kg spaghetti sauce
  • 1 large onion (minced)
  • 1 medium carrot (shredded)
  • 1 red bell pepper (diced)
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1 tsp sugar

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C
  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  3. Start making the pasta sauce by browning the ground beef in a pan. It will release its natural oil so no need to put any. Once this happens, add in the onion and cook for about 15 mins.
  4. Mix in the carrots and red bell pepper, then cook for another 15 mins or until the beef has turned dark brown.
  5. Pour in the spaghetti sauce, sugar, and beef bouillon cube, then let the sauce simmer for 30-40 mins.
  6. While the red sauce is cooking, start making the beschamel sauce by melting butter in a saucepan on low fire.
  7. Once the butter is bubbling, whisk in the flour.
  8. Pour in the milk portion by portion while continuing to whisk. Mmake sure to put in more milk only when the mixture has been fully incorporated.
  9. Taste with salt and nutmeg and let it simmer for about 5 mins.
  10. Add in the parmesan cheese and continue to heat until desired texture is achieved.
  11. To start assembling, mix the pasta with cooked meat sauce.
  12. Transfer to a baking tray/ casserole.
  13. Top with the bechamel sauce and pop in the oven for 30-40 mins. Broil during the last couple of mins for a nice brown finish. Serve warm.
