Drinks
Watermelon cucumber iced tea
Jump to recipe!
TIME
1 1/2 hour
YIELD
8 glasses
You would think that iced tea can’t get any better than it already it because, honestly, it tastes amazing, but you’re wrong! Watermelon cucumber iced tea adds a fruity twist to your classic iced tea, and nothing can beat a fruit + fresh iced tea combination. Though this drink requires a bit more work than others, it is totally worth it and you should definitely give it a shot:)
What you need
To make this drink, you’re going to need a blender or food processor for the watermelon. Aside from this, you’ll also need a medium sized pot, and a large strainer.
Some extra tips for making melon juice!
When you cut your watermelon, try your best to get them as small as possible, because it is much easier to blend it up when the chunks are smaller.
Watermelon Cucumber Iced Tea Recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1/2 watermelon, cut into small chunks
  • 5 cups water
  • 5 black tea bags
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin slices
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  1. Place water into a large pot, then heat it up until boiling.
  2. Remove from heat once done, then add your tea bags and cucumber. Let it infuse for about 15-20 mins.
  3. While waiting, blend your watermelon in a blender or food processor until liquid-y.
  4. Add it into a pot, and mix in your sugar with the watermelon.
  5. Heat up the watermelon mixture over low heat for about 3 mins, making sure to stir continuously.
  6. Remove from heat, then strain out the watermelon meat, making sure to collect all of the watermelon juice that drains out.
  7. Once both mixtures are done, combine them in a pitcher, then let it chill for about an hour.
  8. After chilling, serve with ice and enjoy!
