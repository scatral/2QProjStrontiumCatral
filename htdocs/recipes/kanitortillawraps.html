Kani tortilla wraps
Jump to recipe!
TIME
15 mins
YIELD
6 servings
Kani tortilla wraps is a simple wrap that has veggies, some protein, and kani! It's a quick recipe that you could probably whip up in less than 15 mins, but even with its simplicity, it tastes amazing and is one of my favorite things to eat as an appetizer during meals:)
What you need
All you really need to make this recipe is a plate and a knife, then all the other steps just involve you using your hands.
Some extra tips for making kani tortilla wraps!
This is a very versatile recipe, and you can add a lot of other proteins or vegetables so that the wrap can fit to your liking. I personally like to add some cut up chicken fingers to my wrap instead of chicken breast, and replace the mayo with some ranch or even salsa:)
Kani Tortilla Wraps Recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 pack of tortilla wraps
  • 1 bag of lettuce
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 mango
  • 1 pack of kani
  • Kewpie (or any mayonnaise available)
  • Bioled chicken breast (optional)
  1. Cut up mango and cucumber into thin slices that are about 2-3 inches long.
  2. Shred kani and chicken breast (if using) into thin strips.
  3. Once ingredients are prepared, lay down a tortilla wrap on a plate.
  4. First, put 1-2 leaves of lettuce on the wrap.
  5. Add prepared cucumber, mango, kani, and chicken breast.
  6. Spread mayonnaise on top, then serve and enjoy!
