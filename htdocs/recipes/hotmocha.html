Hot mocha
Jump to recipe!
TIME
15 mins
YIELD
1 glass
Be it a way to warm you up on a cold day, or a source of caffeine to kickstart your day, a cup of hot mocha is definitely a go-to comfort drink. The sweetness of chocolate, and the bitterness of coffee, all partnered up in one drink gives you a drink you surely would want more of.
What you need
All you need to make a good cup of hot mocha some measuring spoons and something to heat up your milk, then you’re all good to go!
Some extra tips for making hot mocha!
Honestly, making mocha is probably the simplest recipe on the site, and I don’t really have any extra tips to make it (which is even more reason for you to try it!).
Hot Mocha Recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee
  • 4 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp hot water
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Place your milk in a pot, and heat it up until it almost boils.
  2. While waiting, mix together the instant coffee, cocoa powder, and sugar in a mug.
  3. Add the hot water to the mixture, then mix until everything hsa dissolved and as well combined.
  4. Take your milk off the heat, and then place it in a jar or any airtight container. Shake for about 30 secs, or until it becomes frothy.
  5. Add the milk to your coffee mixture, mix well, and enjoy!
