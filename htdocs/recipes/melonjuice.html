Melon juice
Jump to recipe!
TIME
30 mins
YIELD
6 glasses
You can never go wrong with a fresh glass of melon juice, partnered with some melon meat! Especially in a tropical country like the Philippines, any refreshing fruit juice is a go-to drink on a hot day. All you need is a melon, a couple of tools, and you’re all set!
What you need
You need to have a melon grater on hand to scrape off the melon meat and make the noodle-like shapes that can be seen in the photo, and also a relatively large strainer in order to get out all the juice from the seeds of the melon.
Some extra tips for making melon juice!
Always make sure your workspace is clean and wide when making melon juice. You’ll need a decent amount of tools to make some top notch melon juice, so try to make as much space as you can because it can really help to ease up the process.
Melon Juice Recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1/2 melon
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to your liking)
  1. Get your melon half, and then scrape out all the seeds and place them in a separate bowl.
  2. Place the seeds in a large strainer, and strain out all of the juices from then and save them for later.
  3. After getting the juices from the seeds, start scraping out the melon meat from the rest of melon half
  4. Add the juice that you gathered and the melon meat that you scraped out into a large pitcher, then add in the sugar
  5. Mix thoroughly until the sugar has dissolved. Feel free to adjust the amount of sugar that you add to fit to your liking.
  6. Place the juice in a fridge and let it chill, then serve and enjoy!
