My Top Food Channels and Blogs
Since I've been baking for so long, I have come across numerous food channels and websites where I research about different food and look for inspiration for my next creation. Here's a list of the 5 ones that I always go back to every single time.
Yeast mode
Yeast mode is an amazing youtube channel that features loads of recipes that range from siopao to cakes. However, I usually go to this channel to find inspiration for my bread recipes! When I try to experiment with new breads and look for recipes online, I have always found that the recipes on this channel always turn out amazing. If you’re looking for a channel to get new bread recipes from, definitely check yeast mode out:)
Preppy kitchen
Preppy kitchen, starring John Kanell, is an amazing place to look for desserts, savory dishes, breads, and much more! Aside from the fact that the recipes here are really good, John also adds a lot of tips on how to make various food in his videos, which is why a lot of my baking knowledge came from here! He has a variety of content on his channel, and even if you aren’t planning on baking everything, just watching his videos are already super entertaining:)
Allrecipes
Allrecipes is a website that has a plethora of recipes, and they are all contributed by different people. Aside from the fact that they have dozens of recipes for just one dish, a lot of people comment on each of these recipes and gives loads of insights. When I get recipes form this page, I heavily rely on the comments that the users give since, more often than not, all the suggestions that they add work wonders and make the recipe so much better.
Apron
Apron is another incredible channel whose bread and dessert recipes are amazing. Aside from the fact that the videos here are super satisfying, a lot of the recipes that I’ve tried from this channel turn out amazing! Aside from this, a lot of the recipes that they put up are really unique and interesting, which is another reason why I love this channel:)
Featr
Featr is a Philippines-based food channel whose contents range from your traditional recipe videos, to food vlogs from all around the country. Though their content is not mostly focused on recipes, they have a lot of very insightful videos on food and is where I get inspiration for what to bake next:)
